How it works

1. We provide a customizable CallMe Button or page that can be inserted into the HTML of your website.

2. Visitors click the CallMe Button on your site to request a free phone call.

3. Our service rings one of your sales agents, then automatically places an outbound call in less than 30 seconds to the prospect or customer

4. Your sales agent is able to engage the website visitor and call records can be integrated with your CRM.
